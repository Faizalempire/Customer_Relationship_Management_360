##### \# CRM360 - Customer Relationship Management System

##### 

##### CRM360 is a full-stack Customer Relationship Management platform designed to help organizations manage customers, leads, tasks, follow-ups, and business activities efficiently.

##### 

##### The system provides role-based access control for Admins, Sales Managers, and Sales Executives with a modern dashboard interface and secure backend APIs.

##### 

##### \---

##### 

##### \## 🚀 Features

##### 

##### \### Authentication \& Authorization

##### \- JWT-based authentication

##### \- Secure login and registration

##### \- Role-based access control

##### &#x20; - Admin

##### &#x20; - Sales Manager

##### &#x20; - Sales Executive

##### 

##### \### Customer Management

##### \- Create, update, delete customers

##### \- Search customers

##### \- Pagination and sorting

##### \- Customer assignment

##### \- Activity tracking

##### 

##### \### Lead Management

##### \- Lead creation and tracking

##### \- Lead assignment

##### \- Lead status management

##### 

##### \### Task Management

##### \- Create and assign tasks

##### \- Track task status

##### \- Manage deadlines

##### 

##### \### Follow-up Management

##### \- Schedule follow-ups

##### \- Track customer interactions

##### 

##### \### Notifications

##### \- User-specific notifications

##### \- Notification management

##### \- Real-time notification support using Socket.IO

##### 

##### \### Dashboard \& Analytics

##### \- Business overview dashboard

##### \- Customer statistics

##### \- Lead analytics

##### \- Activity monitoring
